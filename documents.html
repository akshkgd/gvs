Know more about Gramin Vikas Sansthan

Gramin Vikas Sansthan is a non-governmental organization committed to promoting rural development in India. We believe that poverty is a major obstacle to the well-being of individuals and communities. Therefore, we strive to alleviate poverty and improve the lives of those in need.

One organization that shares our commitment to social impact is GiveIndia (Give Foundation). GiveIndia was founded in 2000 with the goal of reducing poverty in India by implementing impactful social initiatives. Their platform allows individuals and institutions to donate funds to chosen causes and supports programs with high-impact outcomes throughout India.
